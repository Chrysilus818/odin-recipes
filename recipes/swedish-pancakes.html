Do you like crepes?

If you answered yes to the above question, you must try Swedish pancakes. Think of them like a crepe, but lighter and more fluffy. If that sounds appealing, continue reading and I will show you how to make them.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Cooking Instructions

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl with mixer until thick and lemon colored, usually 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in milk.
  2. Sift flour, sugar, and salt together in a bowl. Add to egg mixture; mix until batter is smooth.
  3. Grease a pan with butter, head over medium heat. Drop tablespoons of batter on the pan and spread to make thin pancackes. Cook until light brown on bottom, usually 1 to 2 minutes. Flip and continue cooking for another 1 to 2 minutes. Repeat until batter is used.
